About

When I read traditional success and career articles in my 20s, I was bombarded with the same advice: Choose one thing. Focus and do one thing so well you become known as the best. I have never got to be that person. There is something inside me that does not let me focus on one thing. Is not that I had no idea of what to study, but many. It was not until I found the self-taught path that fulfilled my soul, enabled to mix, merge and select all the content available out there to be discovered, at my own rhythm.
Borned with a deep passion for numbers, commerce, technology, sports and art, found myself with a curious career working as a Full Stack Software Developer, with 6 years' experience in the E-commerce Retail sector, had a short-lived retail startup before moving to Barcelona, and worked as a professional photographer for more than 10 years.
I still have a lot to learn from future mistakes, projects and people. But I can tell you that if you are willing to take this path, sometimes frustrating and uncertain, you will be able to shape yourself with a dash of originality.
